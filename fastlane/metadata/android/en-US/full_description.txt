A minimalist Android app for JPEG compression using high-quality encoders.

Features:
- Browse and select image folders
- Quality slider (70-95)
- Preview estimated savings before compression
- EXIF metadata preservation option
- PNG to JPEG conversion option
- Batch processing with progress notification

Encoders:
- mozjpeg: Mozilla's optimized JPEG encoder
- jpegli: Google's high-quality JPEG encoder from libjxl

This app requires no internet access and processes all images locally on your device.
